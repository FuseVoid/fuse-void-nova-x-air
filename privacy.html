Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 25, 2026

NOVA-X AIR is a professional MIDI control app for iPad. Your projects, mappings, and performance data stay on your device — not on our servers.

Summary

We do not collect personal data. We do not use analytics, advertising trackers, or cookies. We do not sell data. The app works without an account.

Information we collect

We do not collect personal information from you. NOVA-X AIR does not require sign-in or cloud sync to operate.

We do not receive your name, email, location, contacts, or usage analytics through the app.

Data stored on your device

The following stays locally on your iPad unless you choose to export or share it:

  • Project slots, clip mappings, and sequencer patterns (up to 126 slots)
  • Mode settings, MIDI routing, and performance configuration
  • MIDI files or exports you create from the app

Deleting the app removes local app data from your device, subject to iOS behavior.

MIDI & connectivity

NOVA-X AIR sends MIDI to devices and apps you connect (for example Ableton Live via CoreMIDI or virtual ports). We do not intercept or store that MIDI traffic on external servers.

No tracking

NOVA-X AIR does not use third-party analytics, crash SDKs, ad networks, or social tracking pixels. This website does not use cookies for tracking or profiling.

Website

This site is hosted on GitHub Pages. The host may process basic server logs for security and delivery. We do not use those logs to identify or profile visitors.

App Store purchases

If NOVA-X AIR is purchased on the App Store, payment is handled by Apple. We do not receive your payment card details. See Apple Privacy Policy.

Children

NOVA-X AIR is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

Policy updates

We may update this page when the app or legal requirements change. The date above will be revised. Continued use means you accept the updated policy.
